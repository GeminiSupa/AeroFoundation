## 🚀 How to Use

### Add Your First User:

1. **Login as admin**
2. **Go to Users page**
3. **Click "Add User"**
4. **Fill in the form:**
   - Full Name: John Doe
   - Email: john@school.com
   - Role: student
   - Password: student123
5. **Click "Add User"**

**Done! User created and can login immediately.**

### Manage Finance:

1. **Go to Finance page**
2. **See real statistics**
3. **Click "Record Payment"**
4. **Fill in payment details**
5. **Submit**

**Done! Payment recorded in database.**
